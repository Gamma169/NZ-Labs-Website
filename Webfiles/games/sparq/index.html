Energizing the Future!

Energize Sparq

Controls

Move: WSAD keys
Mouse to change direction and Click to Spark
You are invulnerable when you spark, and can destroy the boxes to score points
Note that you can only spark three times before you have to recharge

About

Energize Sparq is a small game developed in about 16 hours over the course of HackDuke. The game is unfinished, but has its gameplay down. More Information and background regarding the game will be out shortly.
