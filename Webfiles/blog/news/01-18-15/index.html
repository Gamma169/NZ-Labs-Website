Energize Laboratories Launches!

01/18/2015

We are very pleased to announce that the Energize Laboratories website has finally launched! After ruminating in Rienzi's head for far too long, he finally sat down for a few days and built this site from scratch.

The website is dedicated to bringing to life and spreading his ideas in a public medium. His main project, currently, is the video game Spectrum, and you should be sure to check out the site's page for updates on Spectrum as the game gets slowly built up in the coming months.
