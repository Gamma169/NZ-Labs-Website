Dorm Room Castle

My dorm room castle project came about due to a series of interesting events. My room had a bunch of problems with it, and as I tried to rectify them, I ran into resistance from Columbia Housing. So instead of just giving in and not having anything, I decided to build a full castle in my room! I hoped to have it show a side of my creativity and surround myself with my passions and interests.
